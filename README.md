# Week 1 

​In this first week I decided on making a Steven Universe visual novel because it is a franchise I am familiar with and really like. During this week I planned the story and outcomes of the story. In the game you are playing as Steven Universe and have to stay at home with Amethyst as your babysitter. In the game you have the choice to either play a game or eat something. You can play rock paper scissors or you can eat a famous Cookie Cat. At the end the Gems come back and you can choose how the babysitting went.

# Week 2

This week I added to my Steven Universe Visual Novel by adding more choices, dialogue and new endings depending on your happiness level and the amount of time you win rock paper scissors. I also added some randomized background music that plays on a loop when the game starts as well as some sound effects for some choices when needed. Lastly, I changed the background scene of the game.​
